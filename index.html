my fucking story

honestly, i built queer grid because i was lonely as fuck. i was tired of watching another tech revolution happen without us. every single time.

we've been here before. when the internet happened, when social platforms blew up, when crypto made millionaires. not this time, bbs.

this time feels different because the technology is moving so fast, but the patterns are the same. the wealth distribution, the access, the communities forming around it - it's all happening in spaces that weren't built for us. so i thought, fuck it, let's build our own.

queer innovation hits different because we're not just building for profit - we're building for survival, for community, for the future we actually want to live in. this is where you find your people and remember that our creative chaos is exactly what breaks systems and builds better ones.
